Cream Cheese Pound Cake

Description

This cream cheese pound cake recipe is rich, decadent, crowd-pleasing, and easy to make with ingredients you likely already have on hand.

Ingredients

These are the six simple ingredients youâ€™ll need to make this cream cheese pound cake:

  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 3 cups of white sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • flour
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and cream cheese together, then gradually beat in the sugar.
  2. Beat in the eggs two at a time, then mix in the flour and vanilla.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
